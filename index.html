LocaliZoom things, under construction
  • Demo dataset is hosted on ebrains. At the moment this "Data Proxy" interface is capable of downloading a single file (double-click, or right-click and "Download Files"), or downloading the entire set. The latter happens via the Actions menu, "Download Bucket" item. Be careful what you wish for, it will download a 4.3 gigabyte .zip file, with 1.6 million files inside. Link showing the numbers: "stat".
